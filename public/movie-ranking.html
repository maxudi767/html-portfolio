My Top Movies of All Time

Interstellar

This is my favourite movie. It is one of those films that makes
you think about everything we have in our life.

Shawshank Redemption

An epic movie about unfair imprisonment of young financial analyst
who successfully escapes the prison.

Avatar

A science fiction movie that immerses you into a new world where
all actors and actions seem so real that you almost believe in it.
